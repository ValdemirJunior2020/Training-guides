Payment IVR

The payment IVR will be used by voice agents to capture payment details from the guest. This allows the guest to enter their credit card information without speaking it to the agent.

There are 3 different ways for the guest to enter their credit card information:

  • Type it into their phone
  • Receive a Text Message to click on and Enter it
  • Say it
