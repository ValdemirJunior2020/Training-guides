1.5. Vouchers

A voucher is HotelPlanner credit, used to apply funds toward a new reservation made on our platform.

  • When Guest cancels with or without penalty and they want to rebook without paying again.
  • When Hotel approved a cancellation with or without penalty for a Non-Refundable RSV.
  • A Ticket then should be submitted on behalf of the Guest with a Voucher Request.
  • Vouchers are sent to guests via email and text message to the email address and phone number on the original itinerary.
  • Vouchers contain a 16-digit number (similar to a credit card number) and a 6-digit PIN.
  • Immediate voucher requests should be sent through Slack “General” channel (same-day re-booking).

Validity:

  • Voucher will be valid for one (1) year from the date of issuance.
  • Vouchers can only be used for Pay Now type of RSVs.
  • If the reservation amount is greater than the voucher amount, guests will be able to pay the difference on the check-out page.
  • If the unused voucher amount is greater than $50 USD, a new voucher for the difference will be automatically sent to the email address on file.

General Guidelines:

  • The voucher is used as a gesture of goodwill for issues beyond our control.
  • Always offer the voucher as a first option, over a refund to the credit card.
  • Guest redeeming their voucher can do so on voucher.hotelplanner.com. Agents can book a guest using the voucher on their agent view.
  • Click “+ Use A Voucher” to add voucher information on the payment screen.

If there is more than $50 USD remaining on the voucher after the sale is completed, a new voucher will be automatically issued and sent to the guest’s email.
